Summary

It was an individual assignment in the fourth semesters. It required to create of a brand for a coffee store. Begins from named to branding system and researching to marketing planning.

Brand System

Named: Flow Coffee

  • 1, The smell of coffee flowing in the air；
  • 2, Time flows slowly；
  • 3, The coffee in the cup is flowing under the lights；

Flow Coffee represents a more relaxed lifestyle. You can follow your nose and try a cup of rich coffee that stirs the senses with strong flavours. You just enjoy your time with your friend to make full use of leisure time to read your book or do your work, there is no relationship with the outside world and not be aware of the time passing.
